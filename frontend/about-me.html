About me

Resilience, Reinvention, and Real Growth

From baking chinchin during the lockdown to building CEO-level people reports, my journey reflects resilience, reinvention, and real growth.

I’m an Economics graduate turned HR & Data enthusiast, passionate about solving workplace problems with both empathy and insight. I have navigated diverse paths, from launching a baking business during COVID to learning how to code, earning certifications in HR, data analytics, soft skills, and accounting, all while building global adaptability.

In 2023, after graduating from Obafemi Awolowo University, I found clarity. My sweet spot lies at the intersection of people, data, and strategy.

Since then, I have:

  • Created a CEO-level People Report using HR data to uncover trends in engagement, diversity, and workforce planning.
  • Explored tools like company software, SQL, PowerPoint, Excel, Power BI, and people reporting to tell compelling workforce stories.
  • Continued self-driven learning in growth marketing, data analysis, and people development.

What I bring:

  • A storyteller’s heart plus an analyst’s mind.
  • A strategic lens on people data and workforce trends.
  • Adaptability across global work, international relocation, and fast-changing environments.

I’m currently open to roles in HR Analytics & People Strategy, Data Analytics, Growth Marketing, Business/Financial Analysis, and Accounting. I welcome all job and internship opportunities.
